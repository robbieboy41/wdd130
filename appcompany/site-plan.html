People will come to my site because I will show specific examples of the applications that my team have created and have testimonies from people that we have helped. We plan on keeping the website clean and professional so that it attracts people the way that it is supposed to. Plan on keeping the website organized and simple to use. Plan on also making sure that we show all our pricing and information so that are visible and up front to everyone so that are as transparent as we can be. All the work we put into it should bring lots of people to the site.
